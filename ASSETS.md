# External Assets

This project includes external assets that are free to use.

- `public/assets/audio/oga-*.wav`

  - License: CC0
  - Page: https://opengameart.org/content/gunshots

- `public/assets/models/kenney-blaster/*.glb`
  - Source: Kenney, "Blaster Kit"
  - License: Creative Commons CC0
  - Page: https://kenney.nl/assets/blaster-kit

- `public/assets/models/kenney-characters/*`
  - Source: Kenney, "Animated Characters 2"
  - License: Creative Commons CC0
  - Page: https://opengameart.org/content/animated-characters-2

- `public/assets/models/kenney-city/*`
  - Source: Kenney, "City Kit Roads", "City Kit Commercial", "City Kit Industrial", and "City Kit Suburban"
  - License: Creative Commons CC0
  - Pages:
    - https://kenney.nl/assets/city-kit-roads
    - https://kenney.nl/assets/city-kit-commercial
    - https://kenney.nl/assets/city-kit-industrial
    - https://kenney.nl/assets/city-kit-suburban

- `public/assets/textures/pbr/*`

  - License: Creative Commons CC0
  - Page: https://ambientcg.com/

The full downloaded Kenney pack is kept under `public/assets/vendor/kenney_blaster-kit/` for license/reference files and source asset context.
The full downloaded Kenney character pack is kept under `public/assets/vendor/kenney_animated-characters-2/` for license/reference files and source asset context.
The full downloaded Kenney city packs are kept under `public/assets/vendor/kenney_city-kit-*` for license/reference files and source asset context.
The downloaded ambientCG material zips are kept under `public/assets/textures/pbr/downloads/` for license/reference context.
